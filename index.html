Hand Crafted, musical masterpieces

Premium Materials

Every piano begins with the world’s finest materials. Old growth European spruce, rock maple and mahogany seasoned to perfection at our Kitami Mill. Sand casted iron frames, hand molded to our exacting specifications at our Iwata Forge. Rare German concert wire, individually wound to bring out its distinctive tone. In the hands of our master builders, these materials are fashioned into instruments that are far greater than the sum of their extraordinary parts.

Craftsmanship

The Factory may be the most advanced piano manufacturing facility of its kind. But there’s a special area where there is no production line. This is the Concert Piano Workshop. Where every piece of every piano is chosen, shaped and assembled by hand, using time-honored methods passed down by generations of our master builders.
